My Dog - Casper






















The story of Casper is such, in short, that I moved with him at the beginning of the road when it was about two months of training and education and correct work to remove all the hard past Caspar had undergone, whether it was a trained transfer to foster abuse and being a court dog. When he came to me, he became another dog whose entire world changes and understands that all people above him are good people and that he can manage the world with a quiet and good life. We both got together and got used to each other and today they will have a regular friend who invests a lot in him and gives him unlimited love without conditions. An amazing Caspar gives a lot of love and a smart and educated dog!
